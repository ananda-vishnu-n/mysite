Motor Control

Our advanced motor control solutions harness the power of Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) and Field-Oriented Control (FOC) algorithms to deliver precision, efficiency, and reliability across a wide range of applications. By utilizing modern dsPIC and DSC microcontrollers from Microchip, as well as specialized motor control DSPs from Texas Instruments, we provide high-performance systems tailored to the specific needs of industries such as robotics, industrial automation, and electric vehicles.

Our expertise includes implementing sophisticated control algorithms for induction motors and PMSM (Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors), ensuring smooth operation, energy efficiency, and enhanced performance. From concept and design to development and deployment, we offer comprehensive services to create custom motor control systems optimized for both low-power and high-power applications.
